Social Media Addiction

What is Social Media Addiction?

Social media addiction is a condition characterized by excessive and compulsive use of social media platforms, impacting mental health and productivity.

Effects of Addiction

  • Reduced productivity
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Increased anxiety and depression

Ways to Overcome

  1. Limit daily screen time
  2. Engage in offline activities
  3. Practice mindfulness
